S e r v i c e s

Projects- Planning stage

  1. Architecture
    • Complete Layout Plan & Development of Site.
    • Road Layout and Landscaping
    • Approval Drawings
    • Detailed drawing for each building with plan, elevation & sections
    • Preparation of detailed estimates, budgets & schedules
  2. Structure
    • Substructure design- Detailed foundation design and drawing
    • Complete design of superstructure (depends on project-to-project basis). However, PEB design to be under scope of PEB vendor with vetting to be done by MEC.
  3. Tendering
    • Definition of Project Specifications
    • Preparation of Tender Documents and Drawings
    • Calling of Tenders
    • Prequalification and Evaluation of Bidders
    • Assistance in Contractor Finalization
  4. Working Drawings
    • Foundation Drawings
    • Detailed Elevation Drawings for Project Definition
    • Entrance Details
    • Doors and Window Details
    • Building Services Details

Projects- Execution stage

  1. Scope of these services is defined in complete definition of project.
  2. Project management service is given with a basic objective of providing timely completion and cost control.
  3. For Industrial Buildings/ Pre-Engineered Buildings
    • Preliminary design & design estimate with complete tonnage of building
    • Proposal drawings
    • Detailed design at job stage
      • Optimization on Weight/ tonnage basis
      • Optimization on Cost Basis/Resources
      • Combination of Structural Components Built Up/ Hot Rolled/Cold Formed
    • General Arrangement Drawing
      • Anchor Bolt Plan Drawing
      • Roof framing and Roof Sheeting Plan
      • Frame Cross Section
      • Sidewall & Endwall Framing and Sheeting
      • Crane Beams & Layout
      • Mezzanine Layout & Structural Details
      • Staircase Details
    • Shop Fabrication Drawings for
      • Main Frame Components- Columns, Rafters
      • Secondary Members- Purlins & Girts
      • Bracing- Rod/Angle/Portal
      • Sheeting- Roof/Wall
      • Trims, Clips &Closures
      • Hardware Components- Bolts/Fasteners
    • Shipper list
      • Bill of Material- Quantity Takeoff
      • Erection Drawings
  4. Phase-wise detailing for large projects
  5. Product Development and Product Enhancement
